> Your paradise for books and stationery in Nairobi, Kenya.

https://nathanaelkitambala-dotcom.github.io/bookheaven-ke

## 📖 About
BookHeaven is a fully responsive online bookshop and stationery store based in Westlands, Nairobi, Kenya. Founded in 2023 by Nathanael Kitambala, a Congolese entrepreneur who expanded his passion for books into the Kenyan market. BookHeaven offers a wide selection of books and stationery products delivered across Nairobi.

- **Home** — Hero section, featured categories, 8 featured products
- **About** — Founder story, mission, vision and team
- **Products** — Filterable product grid (Books & Stationery)
- **Product Detail** — Atomic Habits
- **Product Detail** — Think and Grow Rich

- **Product Detail** — Rich Dad Poor Dad

- **Contact** — Contact form with JavaScript validation

## ⚡ JavaScript Features
1. **Product filter** — Filter products by category (Books/Stationery/All)
2. **Contact form validation** — Validates all fields before submission
3. **Dark/Light mode toggle** — Remembers user preference across pages
4. **Back to top button** — Appears on scroll, smoothly returns to top
