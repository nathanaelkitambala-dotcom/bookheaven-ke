# BookHeaven 📚

> Your paradise for books and stationery in Nairobi.

## About
BookHeaven is an online bookshop and stationery store based in Westlands, Nairobi, Kenya. Founded in 2023 by Nathanael Kitambala, BookHeaven offers a wide selection of books and stationery products delivered across Nairobi.
